Security Consulting

PREPARING YOUR BUSINESS FOR THE LONG HAUL.

From risk assessments and compliance programs to mobile strategies and cloud computing, Vie-Tec Technology offers a range of IT security consulting services that help protect your critical data and networks and prepare your business for any challenge.

Our strategic security consulting services include:

Mobile & BYOD Strategy

Mobile technology has seen a shift in the way we conduct business. Workforce mobility opens up new opportunities, but it also presents new challenges such as the risks associated with remote users accessing sensitive business information from mobile devices. Vie-Tec offers expert advice on mobile and BYOD strategy, including device configuration, information assurance controls and management frameworks.

Security Assessment for Cloud Computing

The advantages of cloud-based IT are undeniable…reduced capital expenditure, greater flexibility and scalability, and guaranteed uptime are just some of the reasons businesses are moving their IT to the cloud. We assist you with assessing your cloud options, including identifying any security weaknesses against industry best practice.

Privacy Impact Assessment

As of March 23, 2023, organisations are liable for any breach in customer data privacy under the Privacy Amendment Act 2012. To ensure compliance with this legislation, Vie-Tec analyses your risks and offers advice on how to safeguard your business.

Security Awareness Program

As of March 23, 2023, organizations are accountable for any breaches of customer data privacy under the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL). To help you comply with this regulation, Vie-Tec assesses your risks and provides tailored guidance to protect your business.

Annual Security Program (Technical & GRC)

Whether by enhancing your IT team’s skills or delivering a fully managed service, Vie-Tec supports you in establishing and managing your IT security program. This includes secure remote access, security information and event management (SIEM), data protection, threat management and mitigation, as well as disaster recovery and business continuity planning (BCP).

Software Development & Security

Vie-Tec can work with your software development teams to implement best practice security into your Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) for any technology platform or business application. We also offer regular software reviews and testing to identify security vulnerabilities before they become a problem.

Business Case Development for Security Projects

Security projects often play second fiddle to revenue-generating projects and customer demands, despite their critical importance to your business. At Vie-Tec, we help you prepare a compelling business case for your IT security project, including risks associated with inaction, broad business benefits and the need for compliance.

Part-Time Security Manager

Our expert consultants help direct your security projects and manage day-to-day security operations on a part-time basis. This engagement saves you time and money on hiring, training and staffing, while ensuring you have senior security expertise at hand whenever you need it.

Project Management

Seamless implementation, rigorous risk assessment, validation and verification, and a single point of contact for all communication—these are just some of the advantages associated with engaging a certified Vie-Tec Project Manager for your IT security project.

Offensive Security Services

Identify your vulnerabilities, know your risk.

By conducting highly sophisticated testing and analysis of your existing IT infrastructure, personnel and facilities, our experienced consultants use their wide-ranging domain knowledge to identify weaknesses and vulnerabilities in your systems that may have the potential to cause significant security breaches.

Offensive security testing offers some of the most comprehensive and rigorous security testing of your IT environment and organization as a whole. Designed to mimic the approaches used by real-world malicious actors, we employ a range of tactics which include:

Vulnerability Assessment

Identifies, quantifies and prioritises vulnerabilities within your network, systems and applications. Vie-Tec consultants then provide expert recommendations in clear language to mitigate or remediate each finding.

Penetration Testing

Simulates real-world attacks against your systems to uncover IT security flaws, assess exploit feasibility, and measure potential business impact—followed by a detailed mitigation proposal.

Web & Mobile App Security Review

Penetration tests tailored to web and mobile applications, leveraging deep application-testing expertise to find both common and hard-to-detect vulnerabilities and recommend remediation.

Email Phishing Campaigns

Simulates real-world phishing attacks to gauge employee risk. Vie-Tec delivers detailed campaign stats and actionable insights to strengthen your human perimeter.

Wireless Security Testing

Onsite assessment of your wireless infrastructure—testing strength, coverage, and configuration to uncover gaps before attackers do.

Application Code Review

Comprehensive source-code analysis across multiple languages and frameworks to pinpoint vulnerabilities and deliver actionable remediation guidance.

Social Engineering

Uses phone calls, emails, ID forging, surveillance and more to expose human-centric security gaps in your organization.

Physical Security Testing

Tests locks, alarms, CCTV, access control and uses social-engineering techniques—like badge cloning and ID forging—to simulate real-world facility breaches.
